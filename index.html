Gereon Kremer

Research Scientist in Computer Science
Stanford University

I'm working as a postdoctoral researcher for Computer Science at Stanford University in Clark Barrett's group. My research interests include

  • Satisfiability Modulo Theories (SMT)
  • Techniques for Nonlinear Arithmetic
  • Cylindrical Algebraic Decomposition
  • Automated Testing and Debugging
